<h1 align="center">📊 Power BI: Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard</h1>

<h2>📊 Project Overview</h2>

<p>
The <b>Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard</b> is a Business Intelligence project built
using <b>Microsoft Power BI</b> to analyze e-commerce sales performance and identify
key business insights.
</p>

<p>
The dashboard transforms raw Amazon sales data into interactive visualizations
that help understand product performance, sales trends, customer behavior,
and regional sales distribution.
</p>

<p>
Using Power BI features such as <b>Power Query, Data Modeling, and DAX</b>,
the project provides a dynamic dashboard that enables users to explore data
and make <b>data-driven business decisions</b>.
</p>

<h2>🎯 Business Problem</h2>

<ul>
<li>E-commerce platforms generate large volumes of sales data.</li>
<li>Identifying profitable products and categories is challenging.</li>
<li>Businesses need insights into regional and category sales performance.</li>
<li>Decision-makers require clear visual analytics to improve strategy.</li>
</ul>

<p>
This dashboard converts raw Amazon sales data into interactive
visual insights that help businesses understand performance and
optimize decision-making.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)</h2>

<ul>
<li>Total Sales Revenue</li>
<li>Total Orders</li>
<li>Total Units Sold</li>
<li>Average Product Rating</li>
<li>Category-wise Sales</li>
<li>Region-wise Sales</li>
<li>Monthly Sales Trends</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📊 Dashboard Analysis</h2>

<h3>1️⃣ Sales Performance Overview</h3>
<p>
Provides an overall view of total sales revenue, total orders,
and product performance across the dataset.
</p>

<h3>2️⃣ Category Analysis</h3>
<p>
Shows which product categories generate the highest sales
and identifies high-performing segments.
</p>

<h3>3️⃣ Regional Sales Analysis</h3>
<p>
Analyzes sales distribution across different regions
to identify strong and weak markets.
</p>

<h3>4️⃣ Time-Based Trends</h3>
<p>
Displays monthly sales trends to understand seasonal
patterns and demand fluctuations.
</p>
